Site Plan

Site Name:

Young Men (@Porto1Ward)

• This name represents the group of young men at the Porto 1 Ward, in Portugal.
Optional domain availability: port1-ym.org

Site Purpose:

• The site provides information about the organization, their activities, and an option to get in touch with people on the organization and the local church.

Scenarios:

• Why having a young men group is important for learning about Jesus Christ?
• What is the activities schedule for the year?
• How can I learn more about the organization and the church?
